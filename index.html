About Me

Adam M. Root, a distinguished product management executive, possesses a B.S. in Mass Communications from Oklahoma Christian University and is pursuing a Master's in Digital Media Design at Harvard University. With over 15 years of experience, he has excelled in senior roles at Neighborly, Toptal, FieldPulse, Real Geeks, Zen Media, Tricent Capital, and Respondology. Root's published works in Forbes, Entrepreneur, and other esteemed publications underscore his expertise in digital transformation, product innovation, and venture capital.
