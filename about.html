About Me

Adam M. Root is a distinguished product management executive with over 15 years of experience. He holds a B.S. in Mass Communications from Oklahoma Christian University and is pursuing a Master's in Digital Media Design at Harvard University. Adam has excelled in senior roles at Neighborly, Toptal, FieldPulse, Real Geeks, Zen Media, Tricent Capital, and Respondology. His published works in Forbes, Entrepreneur, and other esteemed publications underscore his expertise in digital transformation, product innovation, and venture capital.

Adam's leadership style is characterized by a commitment to excellence, a passion for innovation, and a dedication to customer satisfaction. He has a proven track record of driving revenue growth, enhancing operational efficiency, and leading high-performance teams. Adam is known for his strategic vision, adaptability, and ability to foster collaborative and innovative work environments.
